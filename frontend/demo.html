Getting Started

  1. Start the Backend: Make sure your voice assistant server is running on localhost:8000
  2. Launch the App: Click "Launch Voice Assistant" above or open index.html
  3. Allow Microphone: Grant microphone permissions when prompted by your browser
  4. Start Talking: Click the "Start Call" button and begin speaking
  5. Ask Questions: Try asking about company policies, returns, shipping, or general greetings
  6. View Transcription: Watch your conversation appear in real-time on the right panel
  7. End Call: Click "End Call" when you're finished
